About Duo Brew

Duo Brew is a franchise café store located in Brgy. San Carlos, Binangonan, Rizal, owned and managed by Mr. Ryzhick Mendoza and his wife. The café began as a shared vision between the couple, who both possess a strong entrepreneurial mindset. With careful planning and dedication, they successfully established the café, which has continued to grow since its opening.

The café’s strategic location near a school has contributed to its steady customer flow, especially from students. Duo Brew is also known for offering budget-friendly yet quality beverages, making it an accessible and popular choice among the student community.

At Duo Brew, we believe in creating more than just coffee—we create moments of comfort, connection, and inspiration in every cup.
